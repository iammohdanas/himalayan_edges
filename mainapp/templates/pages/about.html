About US

Our Community & Environment

Himalayan Edges are the specialists for privately guided backcountry ski trips on Himalayas Pir Panjal range in Gulmarg. Our guide service is partnered with a travel agent for complete multiday ski packages including accomodation and transfers.

We have been in operation for over 10 years and are based in Gulmarg year round. This depth of knowledge ensures for the best possible experience that only the locals can provide. A ski trip to Gulmarg provides a unique combination of culture, adventure and deep snow. For a customised package please submit a request on our contact page for your group or individual bookings

Our Services

Who We Are ?

Himalayan Edges is a premier travel company specializing in tours and experiences in the breathtaking region of Kashmir, India. With a focus on providing exceptional and memorable journeys, Himalayan Edges offers a diverse range of tour packages tailored to cater to the varied interests of travelers. Founded with a passion for showcasing the natural beauty, cultural heritage, and adventure opportunities of Kashmir, Himalayan Edges prides itself on delivering personalized service, attention to detail, and authentic experiences. Whether you're seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures like trekking through the Himalayas or serene moments amidst the tranquil landscapes of Kashmir's valleys and lakes, Himalayan Edges crafts meticulously planned itineraries to ensure an unforgettable travel experience.

What We Do ?

With a team of experienced guides and professionals, Himalayan Edges ensures that travelers receive expert guidance, safety, and support throughout their journey. From arranging transportation and accommodations to organizing immersive cultural experiences and outdoor activities, the company strives to exceed expectations and create lasting memories for every traveler. Whether you're a solo adventurer, a couple seeking a romantic getaway, or a family looking for an enriching vacation, Himalayan Edges promises to curate an unparalleled journey that captures the essence of Kashmir's beauty, hospitality, and charm. With Himalayan Edges, embark on a transformative journey to discover the hidden treasures and untamed splendor of Kashmir, leaving you with cherished memories to last a lifetime.
